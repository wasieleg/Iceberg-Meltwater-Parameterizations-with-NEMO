Barotropic streamfunction fields and their differences between the ANHA4-EJM010 and ANHA4-EJM012 simulations, including statistical significance assessed using Student's t-test.

BSF_Dataset.ipynb computes the barotropic streamfunction for each model output by calculating the depth-dependent relative vorticity from the horizontal velocity fields, solving the Poisson equation to obtain the streamfunction at each model level, vertically integrating the resulting fields, and organizing the outputs into yearly datasets for statistical analysis. BSFT_Baffin_Bay (Figure9).ipynb computes the time mean over 2007–2017 and generates the mean barotropic streamfunction maps for the Baffin Bay region. BSTF_T_test (Figure11).ipynb computes the time mean over 2007–2017, performs Student's t-test, generates the difference map between the simulations, and indicates regions with statistically significant differences.
