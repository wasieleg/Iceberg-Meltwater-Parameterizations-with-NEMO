Convective resistance (calculated to a reference depth of 2000m) and winter average mixed layer depth fields used to assess changes in water column stability of the Labrador Sea between the ANHA4-EJM010 and ANHA4-EJM012 simulations. 
##MLD: **Dataset_Winter_MLD.ipynb** computes the cumulative winter mixed layer depth by summing all January, February, and March outputs from 2007–2017, while **MLD_Winter_LabradorSea (Figure12_b_d).ipynb** computes the winter mean by dividing the cumulative fields by the 198 outputs, generates the maps for both simulations, and plots their difference.
