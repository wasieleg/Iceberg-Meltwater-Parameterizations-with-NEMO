Temperature and salinity transects across **Baffin Bay** and **Davis Strait**, together with difference fields and T–S diagrams, used to compare the hydrographic properties of the ANHA4-EJM010 and ANHA4-EJM012 simulations.

**Sum_Salinity.ipynb** and **Sum_Temperature.ipynb** compute the cumulative salinity and temperature fields by summing all outputs from 2007–2017, while **Vertical_Sections (Figures7&10).ipynb** computes the time mean by dividing the cumulative fields by the total number of outputs (803), generates the vertical sections for both simulations, plots their differences, and produces T–S diagrams.
